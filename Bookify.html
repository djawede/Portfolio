Overview

Bookify is a unique project—an app concept designed by me and my team to promote and integrate Algerian reading culture with modern technology. Basically a social media app for readers where they can read and listen to audiobooks, save and share their thoughts, This project earned second place at the national Hackathon Sila Day and was designed within a two-day timeline.

Objectives

  • Promote reading culture
  • Integrate technology with literature
  • Facilitate book access
  • Encourage social interactions between readers
